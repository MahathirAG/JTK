    Banking

  • Advisory and court representation, commercial crimes, offences against persons and property, bailable and non-bailable offences.

  • Recovery proceedings and execution of judgments including bankruptcy proceedings, insolvency and liquidation and foreclosures.

  • Adoption, custody, divorce, property distribution, maintenance claims and annulment of marriages.
